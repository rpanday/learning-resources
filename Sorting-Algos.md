### Comparison-based Sorting Algorithms:
- BUB - Bubble Sort,
- SEL - Selection Sort,
- INS - Insertion Sort,
- MER - Merge Sort (recursive implementation),
- QUI - Quick Sort (recursive implementation),
- R-Q - Random Quick Sort (recursive implementation).
### Not Comparison-based Sorting Algorithms:
- COU - Counting Sort,
- RAD - Radix Sort.
